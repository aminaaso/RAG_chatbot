AI SEASON  STEP 3 CHUNKING AISEASON
Important: Bad chunks create bad retrieval.
Step 3: Chunking
Large documents are split into smaller chunks.
Why?
Too Small
"Premium members can return items..."
"...if the packaging is unopened."
Missing context.
Too Large
"Welcome to our store. Our CEO founded this in 1999. We 
sell electronics. Premium members can return items if 
unopened. Our shipping partners are fast. Headphones 
have a 1 year warranty."
Noisy context.
Balanced
"Return Policy: Premium members can return 
items within 30 days if the packaging is 
unopened. Headphones are subject to this 
policy."
Useful retrieval.
• easier retrieval
• less irrelevant content
• fits model context window
• better source tracking
